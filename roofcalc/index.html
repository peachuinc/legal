Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026

Overview

RoofCalc ("the App") is a roofing calculator for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

RoofCalc does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All calculations, saved projects, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. No data ever leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. There are no third-party SDKs that collect data.

In-App Purchases

RoofCalc offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("RoofCalc Pro"). This transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not connect to any third-party services or APIs. It works fully offline.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@roofcalc.app.
