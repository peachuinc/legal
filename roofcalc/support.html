FAQ

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Go to Settings > Restore Purchase. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

Does the app work offline?
Yes. All calculations happen on your device. No internet connection required.

How accurate are the material estimates?
Estimates include an adjustable waste factor and follow standard roofing industry formulas. Always verify quantities with your supplier before ordering.
