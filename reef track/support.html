FAQ

How do I add a new tank?

Tap the + button on the Tanks tab. Enter your tank name and volume in gallons, then tap Add.

How do I log water parameters?

Open a tank, then tap "Log Parameters." Use the +/- steppers or tap the number to type directly. Swipe through all parameters and tap Save.

How does the dosing calculator work?

Go to the Dosing tab, select your parameter (Ca, Alk, or Mg), choose your product (BRS, ESV, etc.), enter current and target values, then tap Calculate Dose. The app calculates the exact mL needed for your tank volume.

Why can I only see 7 days of history?

The free tier shows 7 days of parameter history. Upgrade to ReefTrack Pro to unlock your full history with trend charts.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > your Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You can manage or cancel your ReefTrack Pro subscription there.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. It is included in your iCloud device backups if you have them enabled.
